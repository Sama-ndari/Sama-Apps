Privacy Policy

NotifSpy ("the App") is developed by Samandari. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

Data Collection

NotifSpy collects and stores the following data locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

  • Notification content captured from other apps on your device
  • App name, sender/contact name, timestamp, and notification text
  • App preferences and settings

NotifSpy requires the Android Notification Listener permission to function. This permission allows the App to read notifications as they appear. The captured data is stored exclusively on your device.

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to provide the App's core functionality: capturing, organizing, and letting you search through your notifications. Specifically:

  • Notification content is stored locally so you can browse, search, and recover dismissed or deleted messages
  • Notifications are grouped by app and can be filtered by contact or conversation
  • Counters for captured and deleted messages are computed locally

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for any purpose. No data is transmitted over the internet.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. NotifSpy does not use any cloud services, remote servers, or external databases. No data ever leaves your device.

Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

NotifSpy does not use any third-party services. No data is shared with, transmitted to, or accessible by any external party.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the App. You can clear all captured notifications at any time from within the App.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
The App does not display advertisements.
No data is sent to the cloud. Everything stays on your device.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of interception during transfer.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
