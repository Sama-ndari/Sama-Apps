Privacy Policy

Velora ("the App") is developed by Samandari. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

Data Collection

Velora collects and stores the following data locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backup.

  • Tasks, habits, goals, and journal entries
  • Expenses, budgets, and debt records
  • Alarm and notification settings
  • App preferences and settings

If you enable cloud backup, the following data may be transmitted to Google Drive:

  • Backup data synced to Google Drive (with explicit user consent)

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to provide the App's core functionality: managing your tasks, habits, goals, expenses, journal, and alarms. Specifically:

  • Local data is processed on-device to display your dashboards, reports, and reminders
  • Cloud backup data is used solely to restore your data on a new device or after reinstallation
  • If you use AI features (Life Coach, Creative Muse, etc.), relevant data may be sent to a third-party AI API for processing. This data is not stored or used for training by the provider
  • If you configure email reports, emails are sent using your provided SMTP credentials. Your email address and credentials are only stored locally on your device

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

If you enable cloud backup, your data is transmitted securely via HTTPS and stored on your personal Google Drive account. Velora does not have access to your Google Drive outside of the app-specific backup folder.

Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

Google Drive API — Cloud backup (optional, requires explicit user consent)
Google Sign-In — Authentication for cloud backup
Google Gemini AI — AI-powered features (optional)

We do not share your data with any other third parties. No data is sold or transferred for advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the App. You can delete any or all of your data at any time from within the App's settings.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

If you have enabled cloud backup, your backup data remains on your Google Drive until you manually delete it. You can revoke Velora's access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google Account permissions at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
The App does not display advertisements.

Email Features

If you configure email reports, emails are sent using your provided SMTP credentials. Your email address and credentials are only stored locally on your device.

AI Features

When using AI features (Life Coach, Creative Muse, etc.), your data may be sent to a third-party AI API for processing. This data is not stored or used for training by the provider.

Device Identification

We collect your device ID solely for license verification purposes. This ID is not linked to any personal information.

Remote Configuration

We use a remote configuration service to manage app updates, communicate important messages, and enforce license compliance.

Biometric Authentication

Velora supports fingerprint or face unlock for app security. Your biometric data is processed entirely by your device's hardware and is never accessed or stored by the app.

Alarms & Notifications

Velora uses the exact alarm and notification permissions to deliver reminders, habit alerts, and smart alarms you configure. These permissions are used only for features you explicitly enable.

Data Security

Your data is stored encrypted on your device. Cloud backups are transmitted securely via HTTPS to Google Drive.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
